    About Us

    Welcome to Caliber Composites LLP

    Caliber Composites LLP traces its roots from Om Industries as Maharashtra based manufacturer of composite components for Automobile, Medical Enclosures, MHE’s & custom tailor made developments. Also holds specialization in FRP Lining services for pan India location serving over 20 years in same field.. The company was established in 2004 and brings with it an experienced staff management with more than 10 years of Frp knowledge and experience in manufacturing composite components, with a vision to build light weight components, offering an unrivaled quality of products. Working across a diverse range of industry sectors, we have become the partner of choice for clients who include Automobile Manufacturers, MHE Mfg & Various Proto (R & D) Centres.

    We Caliber Composites are composite solution provider for flexible , stronger & chemical inert products made up-of composites. Based in Pune Maharashtra with the roots from Om Industries (Pune) established in year 2006.……

    We provide pan India services for single proto development to production line manufacturing & onsite Fibre-glass & Carbon Fiber coating / lining services for various application to make product chemically stable & strong.
