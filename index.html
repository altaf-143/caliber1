    WHO WE ARE

    Welcome to CALIBER COMPOSITES LLP Fabrication Works in PCMC, Maharashtra, India.

    At Caliber Composites LLP, we are committed to delivering high-quality, composite products for industrial applications. As an ISO 9001:2015 certified company, we specialize in the design, manufacturing, and installation of advanced composite equipment. With expertise in FRP (Fiberglass Reinforced Plastic) and PPFRP (Polypropylene-FRP), our products include scrubbers, agitators made of MS and SS with chemical-resistant coatings, housings, and a range of custom composite prototypes..

    About Us

    Caliber Composites LLP traces its roots from Om Industries as Maharashtra based manufacturer of composite components for Automobile, Medical Enclosures, MHEs & custom tailor made developments. Also holds specialization in FRP Lining services for pan India location serving over 20 years in same field.. The company was established in 2004 and brings with it an experienced staff management with more than 10 years of Frp knowledge and experience in manufacturing composite components, with a vision to build light weight components, offering an unrivaled quality of products. Working across a diverse range of industry sectors, we have become the partner of choice for clients who include Automobile Manufacturers, MHE Mfg & Various Proto (R & D) Centres.
